Fish for Thought

Being a beginner in the tropical fish keeping hobby can over whelming. What size tank can I get? how many fish can I keep in that tank? what fish are compatible? Fish for thought, helps you by making that decision for you. Clicking on the buttons below will show you the type of fish that can be kept in that tank, but also how many fish you can keep, but more importantly the type of fish they are compatible with.
